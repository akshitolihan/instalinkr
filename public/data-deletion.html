Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Delete Your Account In The App

Sign in to InstaLinkr, open Settings, and use Delete my account. This removes your account, campaigns, leads, and stored audit events from the application database.

Request Deletion By Email

If you cannot sign in, email kumakshit15@gmail.com with the Instagram username, email address, or phone number used in the app. We will locate matching records and delete them.

What Is Deleted

  • Connected Instagram account details stored by InstaLinkr.
  • Campaigns created in the dashboard.
  • Lead records, including voluntarily submitted email addresses.
  • Stored comment and delivery audit records tied to the account.

Confirmation

Deletion requests are normally completed within 30 days. We will confirm deletion by email when contact information is available.
